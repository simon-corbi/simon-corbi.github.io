Bio

PhD student in computer science at IRISA. I work on the KIHT project which consists in designing and developing the deep learning AI that reconstructs online handwriting trajectories from the DigiPen’s kinematic sensors (accelerometers, gyroscope, magnetometer, force sensors). This project is in partnership with Stabilo, KIT and Learn&Go.
